# 🍽️ Food & Beverage Sales Analysis – Power BI Project  

## 📌 Project Overview  
  This project demonstrates an **end-to-end Power BI solution** for a Food & Beverage business.  
  The goal is to analyze **sales performance, product profitability, and customer trends** to provide insights that support data-driven      decision making.  

---

## 🗂️ Dataset  
  - Source: Food & Beverage sample dataset (Orders, Customers, Products, Regions).  
  - Contains details about:
  - Transactions & Order Dates  
  - Customer Segments & Regions  
  - Product Categories & Sub-Categories  
  - Sales, Profit, Discount, Quantity  

Data was **cleaned and transformed** using **Power Query** before building the dashboard.  

---

## 🔑 Key Insights & Features  
    ✅ **KPIs Dashboard** – Total Sales, Profit, Profit Margin %, Average Order Value  
    ✅ **Sales Trends** – Monthly & Yearly performance with YoY comparison  
    ✅ **Category & Region Analysis** – Which categories and regions drive sales  
    ✅ **Top 10 Customers & Products** – Identify most valuable customers/products  
    ✅ **Return Analysis** – % of orders returned  
    ✅ **Interactive Filters** – Region, Category, Segment  
    ✅ **Geographic Map** – Sales by State/Region
